# Economic & Mechanism Security

Use this to evaluate protocol‑level incentives and economic safety.

## Incentive alignment
- Check if users can profit from griefing or partial failures.
- Confirm liquidation incentives prevent bad debt.

## Price impact & manipulation
- Ensure price sources cannot be gamed with small capital.
- Validate TWAP windows and update frequency.

## Fee and funding dynamics
- Ensure fees cannot be bypassed or double‑charged.
- Validate funding or interest accrual consistency.

## Stress scenarios
- Extreme volatility, zero liquidity, stalled keepers.
- Large position unwind and partial settlement.
